Our Story

  • First Virtual Meet

    June 26th, 2023

    Our journey started online. Kaitlin was in Santa Cruz, enjoying a little vacation, and Kamatchi was in Sunnyvale, trying to figure out where Chico even was! That first conversation was full of curiosity, laughter, and an undeniable spark. From questions about hometowns to shared stories, we quickly realized there was something special between us.

  • Our First Date
    Van Gogh Immersive Experience

    September 4th, 2023

    One of our most unforgettable experiences together was exploring the Van Gogh Immersive Exhibit. Walking through the swirling, colorful worlds of his masterpieces felt magical, just like our journey. It was a reminder that love, like art, is full of color, wonder, and moments that take your breath away.

  • In A Relationship

    August 20, 2023

    By late summer, it was clear our connection was real. We officially became a couple, and the months that followed were filled with adventures and explorations. Together, we traveled near and far, wandering the streets of LA, discovering the vibrant vibes of Seattle, enjoying the charm of Boston and New York, and soaking in the natural beauty of Oregon. Every trip brought new experiences, shared laughs, and memories that made our bond even stronger.

  • Moving in together

    September 2024

    A year later, we took another big step: creating a home together. Living under one roof has made every day brighter, full of laughter, warmth, and tiny memories that remind us we’re meant to be.

  • Making it International
    Finland, Amsterdam and Tallinn

    October 7th, 2024

    In October, we took our first official international trip together, exploring Finland, Amsterdam, and Tallinn. From scenic streets and cozy cafés to breathtaking landscapes, every moment was unforgettable. A highlight of the trip was revisiting the Van Gogh Museum, seeing his timeless masterpieces again felt even more special when shared together.

  • Engaged

    December 2024

    We took the next big step in our journey and got engaged! It was a magical moment filled with love, excitement, and the joyful realization that we’d be spending the rest of our lives together.

  • Engagement Party

    April 5th 2025

    We celebrated our love with family and friends at our engagement party in April. Surrounded by laughter, cheers, and happy tears, it was a perfect prelude to our wedding day and a reminder of how lucky we are to have such wonderful people in our lives.

  • Our Furry Family Members

    April 28th, 2025

    Katie’s birthday brought the sweetest surprise when we welcomed a puppy, Mucca, into our family! Our little bundle of energy has filled our home with love, laughter, and endless wagging tails. Of course, she wasn’t starting from scratch, our cat, Yeti, quickly claimed the role of “older sibling.” Like any siblings, Yeti and Mucca have their fair share of playful squabbles and dramatic standoffs, but when the day winds down, they’re the best of friends. Together, they’ve made our home warmer, our lives fuller, and our journey even more special.

Now, after surviving long-distance drives, international trips, and sibling squabbles between Yeti and Mucca, we figure marriage will be a piece of cake. Speaking of cake… we’ll see you on our wedding day!
